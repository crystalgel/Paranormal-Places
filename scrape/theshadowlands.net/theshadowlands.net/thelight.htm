This is a page we've set up with the help of the groups listed below to get people who are having  problems with a haunting, ghosts, poltergeist, etc. the help they need.  This page is for only the who really need or desire help ,  whether is just to confirm a suspected haunting or if you need help in delaing with a problem that is ghost related. If you feel more comfortable talking to us first, feel free to do so, We do work for two of the groups listed below and I'll assist you the best I can and help you with contacting additional help also.

The following groups all deal with the paranormal and ghostly problems.  Each organization handles the cases differently.  99% of the groups are completely non-profit and do this as a service to those in need, for research purposes or religious reasons.  Please contact the group(s) you feel may be able to help.  All of these groups are serious about helping you with your problems.  They will give you all the help they can and refer you to others who may also be of assistance to you and your particular situation.   If you want to know how to find the right group for you, read this.

Thanks, Dave Juliano and Tina Carlson
