STATE:  California - Sept. 26th - Campers   at Mud Springs in the
Trinity Nat. Forest
  between Weaverville and Hayfork Town-
  ships reported encounter with a scream-
  ing yellow-eyed Sasquatch that stood
  and watched them under beam of their
  flashlight from across a creek. Tracks 
  were found. 

Idaho:  Bigfoot sighting and track find  
  occured below Borah Peak near Chilly.
  Reported by horseback riders August
  15th 1998.  Investigated by researcher
  John Gleason, Idaho Bigfoot Research.

Oregon:  Colesino, Oregon - Bigfoot 
  sighting by Marlin and Jose Austin.
  Tracks found and photographed. Investi-
  gation was August 15th and 16th.

Washington:  Bigfoot sighting and tracks
  reported August 20th to Base Superiors
  by two Fort Lewis soldiers. Report 
  recorded at Fort Lewis base Aug. 21,
  1998. Sketch was made of creature.

Title: "Officials Stumped" Date Sept.11, 1996  Sources: Ohio State
Patrol File 9/96
Wire news (KIRO Radio, Seattle) 9/9/96
Bigfoot Trails Newszine Autumn 1996

REPORT:  A serious witness reported to the Ohio State Patrol that while
driving down a rural road in Eastern Ohio, he saw a giant stump cross
the road and disappear into the night.  A follow-up investigation the
next morning by State Police turned up no sign of a giant stump or it's
tracks.
