My Submission

By: darren@evered85.freeserve.co.uk

I’ve finally been motivated into writing these events down after trying to study the haunted history of Rodley, the village I live in. I emailed Shadowlands after finding a comment on your haunted places list that stated "England - Leeds - Rodley - Many apparitions seems to haunt the area." and I received a very nice reply from Tina Carlson stating that I should use the message board to post my own stories and ask for help. Here we are though I’m afraid no one will be switching the hall lights on after reading this as they are relatively boring and I also apologise for the length but I felt I had to add a sense of realism.
The house we moved into seven and a half years ago was built in the 30’s, although our vendor suggested otherwise, and has no historical value as far as we can tell. I think we’re the third family to live in it and we’re not sure if anyone had died in it but we do know, from stuff found in the loft, that the old woman that our vendors bought the house from was into local politics. We have found electoral registers from the turn of the century and a collection of old books in various states of repair.
At first nothing unusual happened but this can easily be put down to the fact that we had to wait so long for the previous family to move out that there were only two weeks before our first born was due and this time was taken up with frantic painting, hanging, laying, stripping and lugging.
Some unspecific time later (maybe a year or so) we started to notice odd things, the sort of things that you would ignore if they didn’t repeat themselves regularly and to be honest we’ve forgotten most of them but one sticks in my mind.
It was Autumn and I was stood next to the Georgian door that leads to our living room talking to my partner who was at the top of the stairs that are opposite the door, running parallel to it. I was drawn to a tapping sound that was coming seemingly from one of the panes just below head height (I am exactly six feet tall). The door was shut and my initial reaction was that it was vibrating in the door frame due to air pressure difference or a nearby vehicle but when I put my ear to the glass it became clear that the sound was as a result of something physically touching the pane every second or so. This went on for a minute or so until I called my partner and asked her to come down and listen to it. The instant her foot hit the top step the sound stopped and did not return.
This in it’s self would not go down in the annals and paranormal lore but it was a taster for a couple of the more interesting events that would follow.
A year or so later I was re-reading (for about the eighth time) the Dune series by Frank Herbert and I was becoming a little puzzled by the fact that the one I was reading had been missing for a few days and no amount of searching had born fruit. One sunny day my partner and I were downstairs and my son Luc was upstairs on his own, probably bouncing on our bed when prompted my some of the minor events that were happening at the time I decided that I’d use an idea I’d recently read in a book. Apparently if you have this kind of thing happen regularly asking for the missing item can result in it being returned so I explained what I was going to do to my partner and said aloud "can I have my book back please". Smiling to herself she plodded upstairs and, after being up there for a few seconds, shouted my name down with that edge to her voice that suggested that she’d gone looking for a shoe and found a four hundred year old watermelon instead.
I ran upstairs and found in plain sight my book laying on the ottoman and the foot of our bed. Obviously I quizzed my partner but she swore blind that it was there when she entered the room and has kept to this story to this day. The only other person left was my son who was stood nearby clearly bewildered by the events surrounding him but I decided, wisely, that interrogating a two year old was not going to make a good read if I ever decided to record this story. Nevertheless I calmly asked him if he’d seen the book put there and he nodded. I asked where it can from and he pointed to the ceiling directly above the book. Odd as it seems this is exactly the kind of thing I’d been reading about when the subject of asking for the book crept into my memory. This has never been explained to my satisfaction as Luc was, and still isn’t, the kind of boy that plays practical jokes. He loses thing on a regular basis but never finds them. That’s our job.
Another peculiar thing actually happened to my partner while I was at work. She was defrosting the fridge and had taken the salad tray out and put it on the draining board full of water and Dettox. She’s gone upstairs to potter or some such thing and when she returned the tray was empty, upside down and lying in the sink. The only other person in the house at the time was Luc and he was asleep.
We later went through a period of time where strange things were becoming common so armed with a CCTV camera, about forty feet of cable and a three hour blank video tape I decided to catch the blighter in the act, if you like. The camera was put on top of the TV and held in place by some blu-tac to stop the weight of the cable pulling it down. The VCR was set to long play and everything seemed to be in order so I left the room with the lights dim.
The next day I had the unenviable task of sitting and viewing six hours of relentless grey inaction but I had an unexpected surprise when I found that the camera also recorded sound so I’d doubled my chances of catching something. After a few minutes it became obvious that I hadn’t the mental discipline to watch the whole tape so I skipped forward for a while and, to my surprise, heard loads of loud knocks and bangs. The most impressive of these was the sound of something knocking in to the camera. It was exactly the same as someone knocking a naked microphone. This sound repeated itself when I repeated the experiment upstairs the following week.
Apart from my partner being very wary of the landing some of the other things that have happened recently include me waking up to see a shape looming over my sleeping partner and a similar shape moving past the bottom of the bed. We’ve been woken in the middle of the night petrified thinking we’ve been burgled again only to run downstairs and find a DVD in the middle of the floor with absolutely no explanation as to how it got there. Things disappear frequently, in fact I kept a log of these incidents but that vanished as well.
We’ve come home from a night out and my partner has seen a man shaped silhouette at the top of the stairs facing to front door. She later had me stand in place to test the scale and as far as she could tell it could have been me.
One of the most impressive things that I’ve ever happened to me witnessed happened about ten years ago when we were stopping at my partners parents house which is a seventeenth century weavers cottage. Ruth was always frightened of the landing there (see a pattern appearing here?) so when she went to the toilet, which is at the other side of the landing, she’d rush back to the bedroom. On this occasion she’d gone through the same routine and closed the door behind her and taken a couple of steps when there was an almighty bang on the door. Needless to say she covered the short distance between the door and the bed at a velocity that would have made Einstein wince. We tried to replicate the noise by seeing if the latch clicking into place and a few other things but the only one that came close was as if someone had hit the door about three quarters of the way up, hard with the palm of the hand.
Whilst at the same house we came home one night after an evening with friends and parked the car at the top of what is a short private road right outside the house. My partner got out and left me to apply the steering lock and secure the car while she proceeded to the front door to unlock it. I caught up with her a few seconds later to find that her mum was at the bottom of the stairs rattling the keys in the lock. This wouldn’t have been in any was unusual but for the expression on Ruth’s face. She later said that she was rummaging around in her bag looking for her door keys when the lace curtain on the inside lifted up as if someone was looking out to see who was outside. She assumed, not unreasonably, that this was her mum who’d been waiting for us but this was shattered when the upstairs landing light cane on and her mothers silhouette could be clearly seen descending the stairs so whoever lifted the curtains wasn’t her mum. We’ve since discussed this and Ruth’s convinced that the movement of the lace wasn’t as a result of a draught or anything like that as it was deliberate and familiar, so much so that she misidentified it as someone in the house.
A few other things happened in that house but aren’t worthy of being reported here. I also do have a number of orb photo’s if anyone’s interested though, personally, I pay them little credence.
All the things stated in this email are as they happened and have not been embellished in any way. I would also like to state that I am as psychically gifted as a tractor with a flat tyre and I don’t have a record of mental illness
If anyone has any comments or info on Leeds or Rodley please contact me directly or through this site.
