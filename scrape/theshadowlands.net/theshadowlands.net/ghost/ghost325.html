fence and explore.  Each time, we ran into "something".

got a good look at what a neglected and vandalized condition the place

was in. Every window had been blown out.  The pool was literally

9445 Coral Way, and probably continues to be so.

If anyone else has stories to share about Miami's West Dade Regional

information about the history of the property that the library now

or otherwise), it would be appreciated.

Work on the house and be with the kids for the weekend. Get some sleep on Sunday night. Get up and on me way back to Arlington about 1:30 AM. That would get me back before 7:30AM duty time.

One Sunday night as I was sleeping in the rear bedroom, I woke up. I opened my eyes and there about six inches from my face was on old man staring at me. His face was so wrinkled and cracked. He looked like he was five hundred years old. Scared the hell out of me. I opened my eyes and he was gone. It was that quick.

This same thing happened several months later. Same sequences of events. The old man appeared only twice to me. I still see his face like it was twenty years ago.

Several years later, my time was up with the Army and I returned home, back to Easton as a civilian. Got a real job and worked around the house on weekends.

One night in May of 1982, My wife and little girl fell asleep on the couch while we were watching television. I was sitting on the floor leaning against the couch. The lights were out with only the light of the tv on. The tv was in the right corner of the room just to the right of an eight foot wide archway going into the sitting room. The lights were off in there also  The sitting room lights were off, the shades pulled. My little daughter had friends over earlier that evening. I sent them all home about 7 that evening. All except one. She walked from the right side of the archway in the sitting room to the left. She was about thirty inches tall, dressed all in white. Long hair, Mid calf white ruffeled dress and white shoes.

    It was getting late and none of the parents called me looking for any missing kids. I called out to the little girl but no answer. I got up and went into the sitting room. She probably fell asleep and just woke up. Being in a strange place she was afraid. I turned on the lights but no little girl. I pulled the furniture away from the wall, no little girl. Wow, what did see?

    Two weeks later, I was in the third floor craw space rolling out some fiberglass batting for insulation. I had to pull up the two by twelve by eighteen foot floorboards to put the insulation down. When I got to the back corner of the crawspace, I pulled up the last floorboard. Under the floorboard was an old shoe box filled with letters from the 1884 thru 1889. Also a child's white pair of shoes and a black belt with a scalloped tin buckle. I never saw the little girl again.

    My kids told me they would hear someone coming up the steps but no one ever entered the hall from the steps. Strange house. The house was sold in 1985. Sorry for such a long letter, but I wanted to write about these encounters for years. Thank You for your time.
