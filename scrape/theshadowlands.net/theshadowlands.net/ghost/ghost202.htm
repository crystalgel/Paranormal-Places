A Story to Tell

By: sheeky420@webinbox.com
 

Here is a story that my Mother likes to tell. I don't remember much of what happened because I was only about 3 at the time (I am now 22). When I was first born till about 4 years old I lived in a two family house in Elma, NY. My Mother never really believed in these things but now she insists that she is always being followed. She never experienced things until she lived there. Well there was one other thing but I will get to that.
It started one day when she was sitting in the living room. Our bedroom was in the far corner of the house and me and my older sister were sleeping in the room. The door was wide open for her to hear us. She heard the door then slam shut and ran to the room. The door was locked and she could not open it. She ran outside to the window and we were still sleeping. The she ran back inside and the door was wide open. This happened quite often.
Then one day she was standing in the kitchen when she saw a man standing in our garden looking at the plants. She had no idea who he was but she starting yelling and told him to go away. He never looked up. She got my Dad and he was gone. A couple days later my sister went screaming and crying from the bathroom. She was yelling he's in there with no shirt on!! The same man that my Mother had saw came up through the bathroom vent with overalls on. He smiled at my sister and disappeared. My Mother knew it was him. She had problems with the electricity also. Many times her power would just turn off. She would call the electric co. , the would come to the door and the power would just turn on. As soon as they left the power was off. They got to know here pretty well.
The scariest thing that ever happened to her happened before we left. There was a picture in the doorway of a vase of flowers on a wooden table. My Mother came home one day to find it upside down. The scary part was it was no longer flowers, it was the picture of a lady and her son, all in blue. My Mother freaked. She talked to a lady that is familiar with these things. The lady told her that the picture meant there would be a series of bad luck, not to her but to someone close to her. Not more than a week later her best friends Mother died of a stroke. Then the same best friend was stabbed by her boyfriend. Then she had 3 strokes and now walks with a cane and talks like a child. My Mother always believed.
The last thing that happened to her had to do with her being a child. Shw woke up one night to a lady standing over her bed. She said she was beautiful and disappeared. My Mother started crying and called for my Grandmother. She never thought anything of it until she miscarried a daughter when I was about 16. My Mother already had a name for her. She worked as a Home Health Aid, and one of her cases's sister was a psychic. She asked my Mother about and apparition my Mother had seen when she was a child. The lady knew nothing about my Mother or the woman, but she said she is her guardian angel. She gave her the name Amanda and Amanda tried to reach her once but was unsuccessful. She had died in her stomach. My Mother freaked because he unborn daughter's name was Amanda. To this day she believes that Amanda is there, watching and protecting her.

My Father Came Back

By: NLibra1062@aol.com
 
It was the winter of 1978, a very cold and unusually snowy winter in Baltimore County,  Maryland.   I was 16 years old and living with my mother and stepfather in a two bedroom apartment. My  two older sisters and older brother had moved away from home.  I had the usual problems and dilemas as any teen ager would have.  My father had died suddenly the previous July of a heart attack. I never really knew him because my mother and father had divorced when i was very young and he lived in another state. I saw him a few times after the divorce, but not alot, never talked to him on the phone and he would send me belated birthday cards as i grew up. To say the least, it was not a very tight father-daughter bond.  He never made much effort,  at least not when he was alive.
That changed a little in the winter of 1978.  My bedroom door was always  locked just in case my mother wanted to barge into my room.  I was smoking a cigarette and getting ready to go to sleep about 11:00 pm, put out my cigarette and turned out the lamp on the nightstand next to my bed.  By the way-the lamp was working normally before i turned it off.  I had no trouble falling asleep that night but a few hours later I was suddenly awakened.  Someone was in bed next to me!   I was laying on my side on one side of the double bed and i was facing out.  Someone was laying behind me caressing my back,  not in a sexual way but  in a very caring way.   I was so scared,  more scared then I have ever been.  I froze for a moment to collect my thoughts and knowing that i was not dreaming, i tried to ask, "Who is there?"  I was too scared because the words could not come out, no sound could come out of my mouth.  I finally got the courage to turn around and see, but could not see what was behind me.  I don't know if I looked too quickly or what but i didn't see anyone.  I reached up  to turn on my lamp, it would not go on.  It did not come on and blink out when a lightbulb burns out.  The lamp  just would not come on.  I bolted out of my bed without looking back and ran out my bedroom door and woke up my mother.  We sat at the dining room table in the middle of the night while i calmed down and i told her exactley what had happened.  My mother told me that a month before she was folding clothes in her bedroom when a whitish colored mist or cloud came into the bedroom.  She felt that it was my father and  she swung her arm out as it came closer,  it dissapeared.  I think I slept on the sofa for the rest of the night.
The next day, my mother called and told my older sister what had happened.  She said  the same thing happen to her a few night earlier. However she thought it was a dream and she only woke up for a minute. She was the only one of my siblings that had an encounter like that, my other sister and brother were married and not sleeping alone.
As i thought about what had happened, after i calmed down, i knew that it had to be my father coming back and trying to be there for me.  I even felt the weight going down on the bed next to me and even could sense what he was wearing.  He died in the middle of the night and was wearing his pajams when he died. And why didn't the lamp come on after it had worked a few hours later?  I changed the light bulb the next day and the lamp worked fine. I never had an encounter like that but had felt i was being watched alot.  Not really anything evil or mean but just watched over.  One night when i had that feeling, and couldn't fall asleep, I spoke outloud.  I said, " Dad, please leave me in  peace and go in peace, everything will be okay".   After that night I never had any feelings that I was not alone.  I hope he did know that I forgave him and I knew that he really did care when he was alive,  he just had his owm problems and could not let me know.   This is not a story but something that really happened to me.

My Stories

By: pkrob@ipa.net

My life has been filled with so many experiences--too many to tell here though I would love to. Reading other peoples stories have prompted me to tell several of my own, in short form where possible.
When I was a small child I had a reoccuring dream (or so I thought) of a boy with firey red hair and thick glasses crawling up the hill beside our house and glaring at me while on all fours. I would always be outside and would hide under my mothers car. One morning I awoke with grass stains on my pajamas. I was raised in an immaculate household and was never allowed outside in my p.j.s
Fast forward to 18 years old. My fiancee had just dropped me off, he lived 3 miles from me. There was a dirt road that went straight from his house to mine. About 5 minutes after he dropped me off I had a strange sensation. I was talking to my grandmother and just lost all train of thought and absentmindly went and sat by the phone. It rang it was my fiancees mother. He had totalled the car and the police were at her home and were taking her to the site. At this time we didnt know if my fiancee was hurt badly or not. We knew he had made the call from a neighbors home to call the police and that was all. We drove straight to this neighbors and he was coming out of the house towards the car. I asked him if his side hurt too badly. He looked at me and said how did you know I hurt my ribcage? (he wasnt holding it or anything). Anyway a few days later we were watching movies and he fell asleep with his head in my lap. He awoke,,startled and said "Oh my God, it was you!!" It really freaked me out. He then told me that when he wrecked the police said there was no way he exited that car even more unbelievable that he walked away with hardly a scratch. He said "It felt like someone put their arm around his side and pulled him out." He had the sensation that they were someone he deeply cared about. In his dream he finally saw the face of the person that pulled him out. " It was you he said." Keep in mind that same moment was when I "lost train of thought" and had a strange sensation.
We had a son.  When he was 4 years old to 7 years old he would cry hysterically telling us to "Make him stop touching me" This went on for several years. He is an only child by the way and was alone in the room when this occurred and always at my Mothers home.  He described the "boy" that had haunted me at the same age. I had NEVER repeat NEVER told anyone, much less my small son that story.
We now live in a home that is aprox. 35 years old. We have a boy ghost now. I dont know if it is the one and the same (I dont think so) but he messes with electronics including making vaccum cleaners run without being plugged in. He loves to "peek" at my husband. This is only in the hallway and there is a closet at the end that gives me the willies. I never knew why and never mentioned it.
I have also seen my dead grandfather standing in my sons doorway looking at him. He was his 1st great grandchild and died when he was 2. Only when my son is ill have I seen him. I havent seen him in 4 years now.
I have many premonitions. Always in the form of a dream.  Luckily they havent been too bad. But I am getting more confident now and tell my hubby when I dream so I have some sort of "proof' that I am not crazy.
The most recent being my dearly beloved Memaw passed away this summer and I miss her badly. In my dream one night she called me and said some things only she would say. I had such a feeling of peace. I asked her a question about my sisters new baby. I asked if she could see her, she said yes she is a living doll. I asked why she hasnt come to me in a dream or like Papaw(my grandfather) she said it takes time. That we have to get to a place where we will accept that we have communicated so we can go on and have peace. I have been at peace ever since.
There are so many more, but maybe another time.  I really enjoyed your site and have bookmarked it for future reference. It truly gives me peace to know I am not alone. Thank you for your time.

Unexplained Incident!

By: 196924@one.net.au
 

In 1999 I was temporarily working as a maintenance person in an all boys  school in Sydney Australia. The place is the Saint Spiridon Collage in the locality of Maroubra. It was winter (Summer in the US) at the time and I started the shift at 5 AM.
The morning was very cold and wet from constant raining. I had just finished picking up a large amount of office paper to be placed in the large iron bin outside when just Four meters away from the bin the bottom of the large plastic bag gives in and the whole load is spread across the parking lot by the wind. To make things worse the sheets of papers stick to the wet ground spreading to an area of 150 square meters.
I went back to the office (just 10 meters away) and when I got back, to my surprise there was not a single peace of paper anywhere in the parking lot. I yelled out ""HELLO", thinking it may be a teacher coming in earlier than usual. There was no reply, just silence and myself in the darkness wondering what had happened. To make things even more mysterious, the main gate was open when it was my job to do that. I looked around but there was nobody in the whole school.
The trip from the bin to the office and back again took no more than Two minutes to make and there was no sounds heard at all throughout the whole incident. There was no sound of any garbage truck coming in or of the opening of the large gate. One thing for sure, what ever happened,  was a great help to me because I was running half an hour late than usual. At the time I was thinking how unlucky I was but Its things like this that makes one feels good to be accompanied even at the most isolated time of all. Was it a ghost?, I think so.
Thank You Whoever It Was.....
