A Couple of My Stories

 

By: bryndy0401@yahoo.com

 

My first story is the one I call The Man in the Doorway.

In the early 1990's I had a friend who was renting a room in a house in the Mayfair section of Philadelphia.  The man my friend was renting from was rarely there, and there was a third bedroom that was furnished but unoccupied.

One night my friend and I were out in the city dancing and having fun.  When we returned to his house it was quite late and we both decided that it would be safer if I spent the night.  The owner of the home was not present.  After unwinding for a little bit, my friend went to his room and I went to the third bedroom and lay on the bed.  I was drifting very close to sleep when something made me look at the bedroom doorway.  There I saw a figure of a man.

Thinking it was my friend, I very sleepily asked, "What?"

Getting no response I sort of lifted my head off the pillow and looked closer at the figure, which was just kind of slouched against the doorframe.  Although there was a nightlight in the hallway to the right of the figure, I noticed that I couldn't see any discernable features.  This frightened me and all I could think to do was turn my face to the wall and say a prayer.  When I looked again, the figure was gone.

In the morning my friend swore it was not him standing in the hall and I know it wasn't the homeowner because he arrived sometime around noon and I asked him if he minded that I spent the night in the third bedroom.  He said he didn't.  I then asked him if he had been home and gone back out while we were sleeping and he replied that he hadn't.  I did not sleep in that room again.

Another strange thing that happened to me also occurred in the early 1990's.  A very good friend of mine took me with her to several farms while she looked for a new place to board her horse.  It was a bright, sunny Saturday and we had been around to several farms, soaking in the sunlight and enjoying the country air.

As we drove up the gravel drive of the last farm of the day I started to feel strange.  My fingers began to tingle and, although it was hot in the car, I broke out in goosebumps.  When she parked the car in front of an old shed across from the farmhouse, I tried to act normal but felt weak in the legs.  The owner of the farm had come out to greet us and as we walked past the shed I felt what I can only describe as a darkening of my spirit.  I wanted to get out of there, but didn't want to act like a fool and ruin my friend's chances of finding a boarding place for her horse.

As we approached the gate that opened onto a path to the barn my steps became slower and slower.  Something inside me was telling me not to go through the gate.  I made a feeble excuse about wanting to watch the horses in the pasture and waited while my friend and the farmer went through the gate and down the path toward the barn.  All the while they were gone I refused to turn around and look at the shed, without really knowing why.

Shortly, my friend returned and I all but ran to the car as she bid goodbye to the farmer.  When she got into the car I noticed that she gripped the steering wheel very hard and as she turned around in the drive I asked her what was wrong.  She related to me that she didn't like the farm and wouldn't be boarding her horse there.  I asked her why and all she could tell me was that she didn't know why - just a feeling.  So, I told her why I wouldn't go through the gate with her and the bad feeling I had about the shed.  We both agreed that there was something evil there and that we didn't want to get any closer to it than we already had.  I do not know the exact location of this farm, but I do know that the area where it had stood was later sold and homes were built on the site.  I wonder to this day if anyone in those homes has weird or unexplained experiences.

Thank you for letting me share my experiences.

Hello Again!!

 

By:  arleneodiaz@earthlink.net

 

 

This is my second submission to your site.  After reading so many stories, I find myself remembering how scared I was to go to sleep while living in this one house.  I must've been between 7 and 10 years old.  The house was my maternal grandmother's house, which had been passed down to my mother after she died.  Did I say house?  It was more like a third-world wooden shack - literally!  Four rooms in the house in all - small living room, smaller kitchen and two bedrooms in all.  How did 7 of us live there?!!   Oh yeah, did I forget to mention the outhouse?

Anyway, my grandmother was a medium and was always contacting the spirit world, this before I was ever born.  After her death, my mother, her new husband and all 5 kids move in.  Though the worst horrors I remember in this house had nothing to do with spirits, (having been an abused child) I never had a good night's sleep in that house.  The roof was nothing more than tin planks, so when it rained it made a wonderfully lulling sound that could put most people to sleep.  Me...I heard this, plus clapping, all night!  As I would lay in bed, in between my two sisters, (we shared one bed and never would I sleep alone or on the outer side of the bed - always on the inside!) I would think, that's not clapping, that just rain falling.  But no matter how much I tried to fool myself out of being scared out of my mind, it wouldn't work.  Some of the clapping was so loud.  I would lay there in bed and envision these hands, nothing more than these pairs of hands in the kitchen, the room right next to our bedroom, floating and clapping all night.

My sister swore she saw this woman going up the walkway that lead from the roadside up to my mother's aunt's house (she lived behind us).  She said the woman was dressed in black complete with a black veil covering her whole head.  As my sister was looking out the only window in our room, the woman turned to look at her and called her name.  I never believed her either.  I told her she was dreaming.  Though I never "saw" anything strange, I cannot deny the minute night fell (whether raining or not) I was always terrified to go to sleep and would lay awake for hours sometimes listening to other noises of the night. 

Of course, this was rural Puerto Rico, where there were rats the size of possums in the cornfields across the road from our shack!  So many of the noises I would hear in or around the house, I would attribute to these and other critters of the night.  

My mother swears experiencing some supernatural events in the house while her mother was living.  She was sitting in on a seance her mother was holding one night and was picked up, chair and all, and held up against a wall, while everyone sat and watched. 

I was her firstborn and she divorced my father even before my birth.  She went back to live with her mother and one night, after I was born, she and my grandmother were awoken by my hungry cries.  They both got up and my grandmother picked me up and told my mom to get my bottle from the kitchen.  My mother could not leave the room - there was one solid wall in front of them but no door!  Both women tried to find the doorway and couldn't.  So my grandmother sat back down on the bed and said, When they are ready, the will let us pass.  They waited about a minute and possibly said a prayer and then they were able to walk right through the doorway they both knew was there.  The room had been dark, due to no electricity in the house - we lived by candlelight back then - I am now 42, just to give you a time reference.  Even 20 years ago, most people there did not have a telephone and would receive and make phone calls at someone's house who did.  Many roadways in this small rural community were not paved and every yard had a handful of chickens - so, yes, very rural indeed! 

As a kid, I heard more than my share of supernatural stories.  Some I found easy to believe some I didn't.  While my experiences have been very few and not having actually seen anything, I know that certain people can open doors into places we'd rather not go to. 

I know this is getting lengthy, but I just remembered another house (this one a row home here in the states) where some creepy stuff was supposedly going on.  In this house it was said that the previous owners had found some witchcraft or black magic artifact in their front entrance and this had brought some spirits in.  People claimed some weird stuff was going on. 

A friend of the family was over one night and while she and my mother chatted in the kitchen, us kids hung out in front of the TV.  My mom's friend got up to go to the bathroom upstairs and did a fast walk back to the kitchen - without having gone up!  She told my mom that she saw a teenage girl dressed in a long, white, nightdress walking from the back bedroom and towards the front bedroom, with her arms crossed in front of her chest.  I was about 13 at the time and did not believe her, but she looked white as a ghost herself and I now think, Why would this woman make this up? 

There's more that we experienced in this house, but I will have to share that later.  Though not terribly exciting, I still hope you enjoy the read!
